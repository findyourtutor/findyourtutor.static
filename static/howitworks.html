Frequently Asked questions

Why should I get my private tutor?
  • A personalized out-of-classroom tutoring environment helps you target the weak areas of subjects to get better grades.
  • We have seen students transition from a struggling academic journey to a fun learning experience with a grade increase of two and more.
When can I start my lesson?
  • As soon as you sign up, you will be given access to your personalized LMS and dashboard. There is no fixed timetable for the classes.
  • Whenever and wherever you feel comfortable, book a session with a tutor and get started.
How much does a tutor costs?
  • Each tutor costs within the economical range of £9, £20, and £30 per hour.
  • The subscription has different plans. You can sign up with whichever plan suits you best, modify, or cancel your subscription at any time.
How are tutor matched with the students?
  • Our system uses AI to dissect learning styles, academic needs, and personality preferences to effectively match students with tutors.
  • We aim to curate a unique and personalized learning experience for you.
Can we change a tutor once we have booked sessions with them?
  • We provide two free trial sessions to help you make an informed decision and proceed with the tutor that suits you best.
  • If you still wish to change your tutor after booking or taking paid sessions, you can. Our support team is at the back end to assist you at every step.
What are the trial sessions?
  • The trail sessions aim to familiarize you with the tutor, their teaching approach, and communicating style, whether it suits you or not.
  • The tutor will use these lessons to assess the student’s academic needs, deliver short demo lectures, and provide a lesson plan personalized to the student.
