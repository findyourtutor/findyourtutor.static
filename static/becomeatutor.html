Getting started on
Find Your Tutor is super easy!

A great tutor is someone who inspires. FYT is designed to empower tutors to inspire student growth
while we efficiently manage logistical tasks in the background,
ensuring a seamless learning experience

Fill out tutor application

Your profile is your digital introduction. Fill out all the necessary details carefully. Select the subjects and grade you are best at teaching. Add qualifications, teaching style, and an introductory video. This helps us match you with students seeking expertise you are passionate about.

Upload necessary documents

Next you will be required to upload the necessary credentials for verification purpose. This is to ensure the quality of tutors and maintain the credibility of our platform

Present you skills in an interview

Present your teaching style and subject expertise to our panel of experts. This interview process offers you a unique opportunity to showcase your dedication to shaping young minds

Application approval

Once you have submitted the application and and passed the interview round, your application will undergo a careful review. Our team will evaluate your credentials and teaching style. Upon approval, you will receive an email notification, making you officially the part of our esteemed tutoring community.
